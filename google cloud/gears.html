Gloves

Fight Gloves

Most fight gloves such as Cleto Reyes are stuffed with horse hair, which provides better power delivery. Buy some fight gloves like Winning use speciail foam providing better hands protection.

Sparring Gloves

Sparring Gloves range from 14oz to 20oz. Most are made with durable foam padding.

Bag Gloves

There are a few types of bag loves:

  • Training gloves: mang boxer use traing gloves to hit bags and mitts, such as Winning, Rival range from 8oz to 12oz.
  • Bag golves with high-density padding: these are specially designed for boxers to throw heavy punches on a heavy bag. And they shall never be used for sparring.
  • Old school bag gloves: these are generally thin and lightweight. A boxer can improve rhythm, speed, and accuracy with them.

Protection

Handwraps

Handwraps are used to make fists solid, support wraist, and make fists to fit in gloves better.

Mouthguard

The best customized mouthguard can be made using one's tooth model. A boxer should go to a dentist to get that first.

Headgear

A headgear is mostly used in sparring to prevent a boxer from getting a facial cut, and maybe getting knocked down.

No-foul Protector

To protect the weakest part of a man, should be worn for sparring and formal boxing matches.
