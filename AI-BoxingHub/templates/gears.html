Bag Gloves

There are a few types of bag loves:

  • Training gloves: mang boxer use traing gloves to hit bags and mitts, such as Winning, Rival range from 8oz to 12oz.
  • Bag golves with high-density padding: these are specially designed for boxers to throw heavy punches on a heavy bag. And they shall never be used for sparring.
  • Old school bag gloves: these are generally thin and lightweight. A boxer can improve rhythm, speed, and accuracy with them.
