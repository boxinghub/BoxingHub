Conpetition Rules

Less than 64kg (< 141lbs) - 10oz gloves

3 rounds of three minutes in men, 4 rounds of two minutes in women. And a 1-minute interval between rounds.

Competition Rules

Welterweight 147lbs and below - 8oz gloves

How many rounds are there in a boxing fight? 4, 6, 8, 10, 12 rounds.

Men: 3 minutes per round, Women: 2 minutes per round. And a 1-minute interval between rounds.
